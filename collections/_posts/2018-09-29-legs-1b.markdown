## Routine

1. Legs
  - Deadlift 5x10
2. Legs/Quad
  - Legs Extensions 3x10
3. Legs/Hamstring
  - Leg Curls 3x10
4. Abs
  - Decline Sit Ups
5. Calves
  - Calf raises 3x12
6. Abs
  - Standing Oblique Crunches

## Notes

- If your gym has an hexagonal deadlift bar, try it out!
- If you don't have access to a decline ab bench, substitute with handing leg raises
